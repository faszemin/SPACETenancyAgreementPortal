TENANCY AGREEMENT FOR COMMERCIAL PREMISES

1
THIS AGREEMENT is made on the date set out in Item 1 of Schedule 1 between the:- 1. Housing and Development Board set out in Item 2 of Schedule 1 (hereinafter called “the HDB”); and 2. Tenant as named in Item 3 of Schedule 1 (hereinafter called “the Tenant”).
Parties
2
Whereby it is agreed as follows:- (a) In consideration of the rent and service and conservancy charges set out in Items 7 and 8 of Schedule 1 for the term set out in Item 6 of Schedule 1 and the Tenant's covenants contained in this Agreement, the HDB agrees to let to the Tenant all that Premises set out in Item 4 of Schedule 1 (“the Premises) and delineated and coloured red on the plan annexed which is subject to final survey being part of the building, together with the right of the Tenant and others duly authorised by the Tenant and in consensus with the HDB and all others so authorised by the HDB and all others so entitled thereto at all times during the tenancy hereby created and for all purposes connected with the use of the Premises (i) To use the usual entrances, exits, landings and corridors leading to the Premises; (ii) To use the lifts, escalators and stairs (if any) provided for the Premises or the building of which the Premises form part. The address of the Building is set out in Item 5 of Schedule 1. EXCEPTING AND RESERVING unto the HDB and all others entitled thereto the free and uninterrupted use of all pipes, electric, telephone and other wires, drains and sewer in, through or under the Premises. To hold the same unto the Tenant for a term set out in Item 6 of Schedule 1. (b) (i) For new buildings, the commencement date of the Tenancy is subject to issuance of the Temporary Occupation Permit (“the TOP”) by the relevant Authorities and shall be based on the expiry of the fourteen (14) calendar days’ Notice to Take Possession by the HDB. The Tenant shall be deemed to have taken possession of the Premises on the expiry date set out in the notice. In the event that the TOP is delayed for whatever reasons, the HDB shall not be liable or responsible to the Tenant for any losses or damages arising directly or indirectly as a result of any late commencement of the Agreement. (ii) For a completed building or subsequent tenancy renewals, the commencement date of the Tenancy shall be stated in the Tenancy Agreement.
Demise of Premises
3.1
The HDB allows the Tenant rent-free fitting-out period specified in Item 13 of Schedule 1 (the “Fitting-Out Period”) from the Date of Possession to carry out the fitting-out works, PROVIDED ALWAYS that the HDB reserves the right to demand payment of rent (based on the base rent) for the fitting-out period, together with the service and conservancy charges and other charges that may be due, in the event that the Tenant terminates or gives notice to terminate the Agreement within one (1) year of the commencement of the tenancy or if the HDB terminates the tenancy at any time due to a breach by the Tenant of any provisions under this Agreement.
Rent-Free Fitting-Out Period
3.2
No additional rent-free “fitting-out” period shall be given to the Tenant, if more time is required for fitting-out work.
3.3
The Tenant shall, before commencing any fitting-out works for the Premises according to the HDB’s rules, regulations and guidelines relating to the fitting-out and design works for the Premises, pay to the HDB the Fitting-Out and Design Deposit set out in Item 12 of Schedule 1. 2 The Fitting-Out and Design Deposit shall be held by the HDB as security for the due performance by the Tenant of the following obligations: (a) To make good to the HDB’s satisfaction all damage to the Premises resulting from the execution of the fitting-out works; (b) To remove all waste materials and debris immediately upon completion of the fitting-out works. The HDB may at its sole option, without being obliged to do so, arrange for the removal of the same off-site by its contractor and the costs therefore shall be borne by the Tenant; and (c) To comply with the requirements and abide by the terms set out in this Agreement and the Tenancy design and fittingout guide (a copy of which will be given to the Tenant). The Fitting–Out and Design Deposit will be refunded to the Tenant within one (1) month only upon the satisfactory completion of the Tenant’s fitting-out works free of interest less any amount forfeited due to the Tenant’s non-compliance with the aforesaid obligations.
Fitting-Out and Design Deposit
